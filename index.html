WHAT IS IDENTITY V?

'Identity V' is an asymmetrical 1v4 game developed by the company 'Netease' in July 2018.
It is a free to play mobile game that has a PC version available.
The game style is very Tim Burton/Coraline-esque, as the characters have a doll-like appearance. This is what got me into the game.
The gameplay is very similar to 'Dead by Daylight', where four Survivors go up against one Hunter. Suruvivors have to escape and the Hunter has to stop them.
New players assume the role of Orpheus, a detective tasked with resolving a missing persons case, who was sent a letter to find a missing girl.
The player ends up in a strange manor, finding abandoned diaries that describe a horrifying game once held in the manor
